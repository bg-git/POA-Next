All prices shown are for piercing service only. Jewellery is charged extra unless stated.  
Piercing under the age of 16 will require parental/guardian consent. Siblings are not considered as guardians.

All clients aged between 16 -18 (inclusive) will require valid photographic ID. In some cases, if you are fortunate enough to look younger than your age, our staff withhold the right to request valid photographic ID.

If you require a piercing that isn't on the list please contact us.

Walk-in are welcome, however booked appointments will take priority during busy periods.
